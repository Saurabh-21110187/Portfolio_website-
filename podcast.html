2R Robotic Arm [January 2023]

In January 2023, I developed a 2R robotic arm with two rotatory joints and one prismatic joint, closely emulating the human arm's movements and accurately positioning the prismatic tip based on given coordinates. By programming the Arduino for motion control and integrating it with Tinkercad, I achieved seamless operation. Additionally, I constructed a detailed AutoCAD model to ensure structural integrity and joint stability. Key tools used include Arduino IDE, Tinkercad, and AutoCAD.

Solar Tracker [November-December 2023]

Between November and December 2023, I created a solar tracker device that follows the sun's motion for optimal sunlight exposure. Designing the working structure, I developed an algorithm for fast-tracking and motion replication. Utilizing Arduino IDE, Tinkercad, and AutoCAD, I successfully integrated the system, allowing the solar tracker to adjust its position dynamically in response to the sun's movement.

BB8 [August 2022-October 2022]

During the period from August to October 2022, I designed and modeled a robotic prototype inspired by Star Wars' BB8 character. Focusing on its spherical design and rolling movement, I utilized Arduino IDE to program the control algorithm, enabling the robot to navigate and roll autonomously. Employing Tinkercad and AutoCAD, I created a functional and aesthetically faithful BB8 prototype, showcasing my proficiency in robotics and control systems.
